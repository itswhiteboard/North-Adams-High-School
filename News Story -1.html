Reading Horizons Elevate Training

September 8th, 2022



Teachers from all buildings participated in Reading Horizons Elevate training. Reading Horizons Elevate is a reading intervention program for students in grades 4-12. When older learners have reading difficulties it is often because they have gaps in the foundational decoding skills needed for fluent reading.
