North Adams High School



Description

North Adams High School is a public high school located in Seaman, Adams County, Ohio, United States. It is one of three high schools in the Adams County/Ohio Valley School District, the other two being Peebles High School and West Union High School. The district also has a vocational school (Ohio Valley Career and Technical Center). North Adams High School has been declared by the State of Ohio as a "School of Promise."
